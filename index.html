About

Starting Fall 2024, I will be a PhD student in Computer Science at UChicago, advised by Bill Fefferman.

Recently, I completed my Master’s in physics at Freie Universität Berlin, co-supervised by Jens Eisert and Alex Bredariol Grilo. Before that, I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computing Mathematics, with minors in physics and computer science at the City University of Hong Kong, advised by Oscar Dahlsten.

I like to think about questions surrounding quantum computation and complexity theory.
